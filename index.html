About Rotaract Club of Waknaghat

Rotaract Club of Waknaghat, the youth wing of Rotary Club of Shimla is an institutional-based organisation which encourages and fosters the ideal of service as a basis of worthy enterprise. Waknaghat is a secluded area of Himachal Pradesh, yet the spirited and enthusiastic youth of our club have successfully led to progressive emergence of this place.

What is Rotary

Rotary is a global network of 1.2 million neighbours, friends, leaders, and problem-solvers who want a world where people unite and take action to create lasting change – across the globe, in our communities, and ourselves.

What is Rotaract

Rotaract clubs bring together people aged 18 and older to exchange their ideas with leaders in the community, develop leadership and professional skills, and have fun through service. In communities worldwide, Rotary and Rotaract members work side by side to take action through their services. From big cities to rural villages, Rotaract is changing communities like yours. Our Mission

The mission of our club is to provide a suitable platform for each and every untapped talent of the club by bringing inter-club opportunities to the table and provide exposure to our young Rotaractors.

Our Vision

With the vision of Embrace, Enhance, Empower, Rotaract Club of Waknaghat put forward the Embrace leadership and skill development with the best experience to Enhance the team and Empower every individual to bring out the best of themselves.
