About Event



It's not that we use technology, we live technology. With that thought in mind, we are here to provide a platform for ideas, innovation and creative endeavours. All those things with a kind deed in our hearts!

Rotaract Club of Waknaghat, therefore, presents before you all, for the very first time, Hack-a-care.

24 hours of enthralling ideation, application, discussion with exciting hampers and obviously some delightful activities.

It is a battle to find which among you can arise victoriously.
There will be prizes in categories like best Idea, best prototype, best presentation and much more.
Judging criteria for all categories will be different.
Any category judgment would not be influenced by other categories!

No Need to prepare for anything!
With the start of event, on 28th August ,we will release the event theme. You’ll have to start thinking about a real world problem around that theme!
You will have 24 hours to come up with a solution for the problem and present it to the panel of judges.
You’ll also be given points based on the real world problem you choose!

Rules and Regulations

  • Each team should have a minimum of 2 members and a maximum of 4 members. The members can be from the same/different institutions. Each team must have a team leader.

  • All submissions will remain the intellectual property of the individuals that developed them.

  • The members of each team should not present projects that have previously been submitted to other initiatives.

  • If the submission is work-based on an existing application, the participant should have acquired sufficient rights to use it.

  • The event will continue for 24 hours. The team will give a presentation on their proposed solution after the ideation phase.

  • The organizing committee holds the right to disqualify any team. Any type of misbehavior with the judges will result in disqualification.

  • The organizing team reserves the right to change or modify any of the rules. The decision of judges will be final and binding.
